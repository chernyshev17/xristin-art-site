Oil paintings on canvas

Warm light, quiet stories.

Still lifes with tea and lace, quiet fields under heavy clouds, and a contemporary birch series painted with a palette knife. Oil paintings created in Belgium, with roots in Eastern European tradition.

View gallery Available works

Series

Three bodies of work that speak to each other: intimate still life, open landscapes, and a more abstract birch series.

Still Life

Teapots, lace, flowers and sweets – rich classical still lifes painted from life with attention to texture and light.

Landscapes

Fields, rivers and distant mountains, from quiet grey hours to blazing Kuindzhi-inspired sunsets.

Birch Series

A contemporary series of birch trunks painted with a knife: bold colour fields, strong texture, and simple graphic rhythm.
