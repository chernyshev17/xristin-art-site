About

I am Kristina (Христина), an artist currently based in Leuven, Belgium. I work primarily in oil on canvas, with a focus on quiet, intimate interiors and fragments of everyday life.

My paintings often return to the same objects, corners and light sources — a chair, a window, a tablecloth — tracing how they change with time, mood and memory. Colour and atmosphere are as important to me as the subject itself.

Originally from [your origin, if you wish to mention], I studied [any studies, or “I am self-taught”] and have been developing this body of work over the past years while living in different cities.
