About

Xristina is a painter working primarily in oil on canvas, based in Belgium. Her work is rooted in Eastern European realism, with a love for atmosphere, light and small everyday rituals.

Still lifes with tea, glass, lace and sweets sit alongside open fields, winter villages and a contemporary birch series painted almost entirely with a palette knife.

Each painting is created slowly, with layered colour and attention to surface. Many of the works are available unframed, with the option to arrange framing on request.
